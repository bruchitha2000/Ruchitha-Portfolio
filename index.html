Hi!

I’ve always been curious about the “why” behind numbers, why trends change, why
performance shifts, and what stories data is trying to tell. That curiosity naturally led me to
the world of data analytics.

Today, I specialize in turning complex financial and operational data into clear, meaningful insights.
I enjoy building dashboards that simplify decision-making, creating forecasting
models that bring clarity to uncertainty, and automating processes that save time and reduce errors.

What excites me most about analytics is the impact, helping teams move from guesswork to confident, data-driven decisions.
Whether it’s improving efficiency, identifying cost patterns, or highlighting growth opportunities,
I focus on delivering insights that create measurable value.

I work with tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel, but more importantly, I use them to tell stories.
Because to me, data is not just numbers on a screen; it’s a powerful narrative waiting to be uncovered.

When I’m not analyzing datasets, I’m continuously learning, exploring new technologies,
and refining my ability to bridge the gap between business strategy and technical
execution.
